What is Sentiment Analysis?

The study of extracted information to identify reactions, attitudes, context and emotions.As one of the applications of text mining, sentiment analysis exposes the attitudes in the mined text. It is based on word polarities, it takes into account positive or negative words and neutral words are dismissed.


Positive Neutral Negative
Joy Is Bad
Happy They Disgusting
Pleasent Are Unpleasent

Sentiment analysis is done based on lexicons. A lexicon in simpler terms is a vocabulary , say the English lexicon.In this context, a lexicon is a selection of words with the two polarities that can be used as a metric in sentiment analysis.


How Sentiment Analysis works?


  1. Review is Extracted from the Source.

  2. Extraction of Opinionative and usefull words and phrases.

  3. Feature Selection is Carried Out.

  4. Sentiments are then Classified and thier polarity is determined.

Sentiment analysis, also known as opinion mining, allows us to use computers to analyze the sentiment of a piece of text. Essentially analyzing the sentiment of text allows us to get an idea of whether a piece of text is positive, negative or neutral.
Since the data we fetched was unlabelled data in terms of Sentiment, So We used the pyhon library known as TextBlob to first find the subjective polarity of the reviews. After that, Sentiment corrosponding 1 for positive ,-1 for negative and 0 for neutral is given to each review.
For example, below is a chart showing the sentiment of Reviews we gathered that of target airlines from Skytrax Rating Wesbite Github Repository.



The chart shows the high level summary of the people's opinions towards each airline. You can see that sentiments are more negative than positive in case of spirit airlines wherease sentiments are more positive in case of british airlines.
This gives us a good understanding of the public’s opinion about these certain airlines at the time we collected the reviews, it actually doesn’t tell us much about what exactly people were speaking positively or negatively about.


Sentiment analysis becomes a very active research area in the text mining field. It aims to extract people's opinions, sentiments, and subjectivity from the texts. Sentiment analysis can be performed at three levels: at document level, at sentence level and at aspect level. An important part of research effort focuses on document level sentiment classification, including works on opinion classification of reviews.


The Main Aim of the Project is to compare the different algorithms working on the Document Level Sentiment Analysis and suggesting the future scope of the Sentiment Analysis in the business escalation and its application in the Industry.
